Monstera

AKA the Swiss Cheese Plant

The monstera plant is a vine native to the tropical rain forests of Central America and Mexico. They are popular indoor plants.

Light
Monsteras can survive in low light, but like most plants will thrive in bright indirect light.Â 

Water
Monsteras need moderate levels of water. Not too much and not too little. Lagom!

Care
Monstera leaves can be wiped with a clean wet cloth every once a week.
